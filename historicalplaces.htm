Sazova Park

A green heaven in the heart of the city, the Sazova Park is the perfect way to spend an afternoon at, if you are travelling with kids. Soak up the sun while your little ones run around to explore the castle and the ship or make this a learning experience as you scan through the Science center. A must if you are looking for a relaxing place among green grass. The place also has a cafe- perfect for a light meal.

Yazilikaya

Popularly known as the Tomb of King Midas, Yazilikaya is a favourite attraction among tourists and locals alike. Excavated by the French Archaeological Institute immediately after the Second World War, the place is located 66 kilometres south of Eskisehir. The whole excavated site takes almost 2 hours to explore. A must if you love history archaeology or simply want to have an Indiana Jones experience!

Luletasi Museum

Meerschaum, better known as Sepiolite, is a mineral which is often used to make elaborate smoke pipes. The soft white mineral has the perfect consistency to carve into for making intricate designs. Located at the heart of the old part of the city, the building sits perfectly amidst the charm. At the museum you will find sepioloite pipes which will make you wonder if they are in fact made from marble! Scroll through an array of them which showcase detailing like never before. If you don’t mind spending, gift the pipes to your friends!
